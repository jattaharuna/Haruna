HARRUNA JATTA:

The School Council is made up of: the school director, who chairs it, all the teachers assigned to the school, the mayor and the municipal councilor in charge of school affairs, elected representatives of the parents of students in number equal to that of the school classes, of the education department delegate responsible for visiting the schools.

Mastery of the English language and the first elements of mathematics are the priority objectives of elementary school to allow students to access the fundamental tools of knowledge.

From CP, students discover learning a modern language (foreign or regional). In cycle 2, the program includes lessons to question the world, artistic lessons (plastic arts and musical education), physical and sports education and moral and civic education. It is in educational continuity that actions must be undertaken allowing students to develop behaviors of individual, collective, moral and civic responsibility by appealing to the sensitivity, conscience and commitment of each person. The modalities of action must be articulated with the objectives of knowledge and skills for mastering the common core, in particular those of domain 3 "the training of the person and the citizen", the disciplinary and interdisciplinary courses as well as the different courses educational courses implemented, more particularly the educational health course and the citizenship course. School teachers, school principals and national education inspectors in charge of the primary school district can participate in the health and citizenship education committee (CESC) of the college, as part of Interdegree CESC.
